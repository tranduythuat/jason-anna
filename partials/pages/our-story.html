Our love story

In 2022, Jason and his friend CS went to the famous Raffles Hotel in Singapore to try a famous Singapore drink.

They went to the wrong restaurant and bumped into Anna where she was working. They ended up having dinner there because where they wanted to go had a long line.

With CS’s help and a lot of luck, Jason was able to get Anna’s number. This was the start of our love story.

Anna put in her notice at work a few days before she met Jason. She about to leave Singapore for good.

A few days after, Jason had a business trip in Hanoi meanwhile Anna also had her ticket in advance to set up her new Cafe in Vietnam.

This is our first pics together in Hanoi 2022.

July 2024 - NYC
Our very first pics in NY together

After losing contact with each other for some time, by sheer luck, we became reunited in New York. At the persuasion by Anna’s mom, they booked a last minute trip to New York.
