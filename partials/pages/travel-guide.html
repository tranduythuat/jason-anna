DO I NEED A VISA TO GO TO VIETNAM?

Yes, you need a visa to enter Vietnam.

There are a few options for obtaining a visa, including:

E-visa US citizens can apply for an E-visa online through the Vietnam Immigration website. E-visas are valid for up to 90 days and can be single or multiple entry. The cost is $25 for a single entry and $50 for multiple entries. E-visas are usually processed within three to five working days.
